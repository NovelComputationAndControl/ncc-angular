Aims and scope

The Novel Computation and Control Journal is a new scientific publication oriented towards hot areas in digital computing, that include (but are not limited to) bioinformatics, genomics, big data, machine and deep learning, cyber-physical systems, emergent behavior in autonomous systems etc.

Our editorial policy aims at strongly encouraging the reproducibility of the scientific results, therefore authors will submit online all supplemental materials required, such as source code, databases, etc.

This journal will be published and promoted online, with its printed version also available upon request.
